ABM


This ABM uses agents to move around a 100x100 grid environment and eat the environment.
The agents will move randomly, and move more quickly if they have less food stored, as they are hungry!
The agents will share food with other sheep nearby, but if they have less food stored will be more greedy.
The agents will throw up if their food store exceeds 100.
The agents have a 25% chance of becoming ill, and will be ill for a maximum of 4, until they either get better or may die from the illness.
The agents have a 10% chance of passing on the infection to other agents if they are nearby.
The predators will move around the 100x100 grid twice as fast as the agents and eat the agents if they are within a certain distance of them.
The predators will throw up if their food store exceeds 300.
The predators have a 30% chance of killing the agents if they are within a certain distance of them to eat.
